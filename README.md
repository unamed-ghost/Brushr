A iOS app that helps you maintain better oral hygiene habits by tracking brushing sessions, streaks, flossing, and mouthwash usage.

# Features
- Brushing Timer
  Track your daily brushing sessions with a timer
- Streak Tracking
  Build and maintain your brushing streaks
- Flossing log
  Record your daily flossing
- Mouthwash Tracker
  Keep track of your mouthwash usage
- Gamification
  Earn streaks and achievements to stay motivated and build your routine
## Early development
The app is in an early development phase. The goal is to release it on the Apple App Store.
